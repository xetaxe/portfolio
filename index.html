About

I fell in love with programming during my Mathematics degree, where I was lucky enough to meet other hacky friends. That was the moment in which I discovered the power of the triforce: HTML, CSS & JS. During those days, I recurrently had the chance to use them in several hackathons and side projects, cementing a good base for later learning.

After finishing my studies in advanced mathematics and a brief detour into the domains of data science, I decided to return to and delve deeper into web development. Ever since, my main focus as a software consultant and full stack developer has been enjoying the crazy ride in this ever-evolving field.


My tools and areas of interest revolve around Typescript, React, Next.js, Node, both SQL (MySQL, PostgreSQL) and no SQL databases (MongoDB), and recently Golang. I am also acquainted to Python and its related data science libraries.


When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually playing the guitar, reading, hanging out with friends or learning useless information on Youtube/Wikipedia.
