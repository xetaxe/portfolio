About me

I fell in love with programming during my Mathematics degree, where I was lucky enough to meet other hacky friends. During those days, I recurrently had the chance to use the triforce (HTML, CSS & JS) in several hackathons and side projects, cementing a good base for later learning.

After finishing my studies in advanced mathematics and a brief detour into the domains of data science, I decided to return to and delve deeper into web development. Ever since, my main focus has been enjoying the crazy ride in this ever-evolving field.



I am always excited about building cool websites and web applications. About turning designs into reality, or creating them from scratch using my UI/UX knowledge if necessary. About adding new features, optimizing the backend or solving these nasty bugs that hinder performance.

My goal? Crafting robust but visually appealing and user-friendly applications that provide a seamless browsing experience.

My tools and areas of interest revolve around Typescript, React, Next.js, Node, both SQL and no-SQL databases, and recently Golang. I am also acquainted to Python and its related data science libraries.
