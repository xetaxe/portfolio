About

I fell in love with programming during my Mathematics degree, more than eight years ago, where I as lucky enough to meet other hacky friends. That was the moment where I realized the power of HTML, CSS & JS, which I recurrently put into practice on several hackathons or side projects.

After finishing my studies in advanced mathematics and a brief detour into the domains of data science, I decided to return to and delve deeper into web development. Ever since, my main focus as a software consultant and full stack developer has been building cool stuff and enjoying the crazy ride in this ever-evolving field.

When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually playing the guitar, reading, hanging out with friends or learning useless information on Youtube/Wikipedia.
